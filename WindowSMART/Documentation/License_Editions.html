WindowSMART 2015 is licensed, not sold. If you wish to continue using the software after the 30-day trial period, you must purchase a license. Licenses are good forever, and require no renewal. Purchase a license one time, and all updates are free. This covers both minor updates and major upgrades.

License Classes

WindowSMART 2015 is designed for businesses, but is simple enough for the home. WindowSMART 2015 is available in three versions, Single PC, Home and Professional.

Home licenses are designed for simplicity, and are licensed per residential address. This means you need to purchase only one license, and all of the computers in your home are covered. There's no need to count PCs to see how many licenses you need. One license, and your home is covered. It's that simple. Home licenses also cost less than Professional licenses. There is, however, a loss of some functionality in the Home edition. If you purchase multiple home licenses, you will be issued multiple keys. This allows you to purchase licenses to give to family and friends.

Single PC licenses are, like Home licenses, intended for use in a home environment. The only restriction is that you are allowed to install the software on ONE Windows PC. The advantage of the Single PC license is that it costs less than the Home license, and is intended primarily for homes where there is only one PC.

Professional licenses are intended for businesses. Professional licenses are licensed per device. This means if you want to install the software on 237 computers, you must purchase 237 licenses. Volume discounts are available in tiered quantities. If you require more than 1000 Professional licenses for a large business, please email msawyer91@dojonorthsoftware.net to request a custom quote. If you plan to use WindowSMART in a place of business (other than a home office), you must purchase a Professional license.
